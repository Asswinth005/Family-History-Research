Our comprehensive family tree research traces your lineage using global archives, census records, vital records, church registers, and more. Each fact is verified with primary sources. Our genealogists go back 5-7 generations or more, depending on record availability. You'll receive a detailed report with family tree charts, ancestor profiles, and source citations for every fact discovered.

  • 5+ generations traced (names, dates, locations)
  • 25+ primary source citations with document images
  • Digital family tree chart (interactive PDF)
  • Ancestor profiles with biographical information
  • Research log documenting search process
Step 1: Free 30-minute consultation to discuss your research goals.
Step 2: Comprehensive search of vital records, census data, and archives.
Step 3: Analysis and cross-referencing of all sources for accuracy.
Step 4: Compilation of detailed report with family tree chart.
Step 5: Delivery of final report and optional follow-up consultation.
